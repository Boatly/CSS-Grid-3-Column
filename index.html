Kyle Rollins

I'm an experienced technical writer who loves evaluating, breaking down, and solving complex problems.

My experience covers consumer electronics reviews, marketing copy, technical documentation, and website/UI microcopy.

I've worked with small companies (like Emsi) and large companies (like eBay). At each, I meet great people and learned invaluable skills.

Currently, I work for 3M Health Information Systems, documenting software in the healthcare industry.

As a co-organizer for the Write the Docs SLC meetup, I get to meet and learn from amazing people on a regular basis. I'm honored to facilitate knowledge sharing in the local community.
