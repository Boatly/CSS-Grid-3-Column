Work philosophy

Good communication is hard. Good communication through text-based mediums is particularly difficult. You need to take your chaotic information and package it in a way that someone else will understand it.

The first step to good communication is patience. Take the time to understand who you're communicating with and what they need. This can be through empathy research, usage analytics, or any number of other research methods.

The next step? Don't waste time. You get little time to convince people that you have the answers they're looking for.

In everything, be kind, courteous, and understanding. Be open to criticism, as it's one of the best ways to learn. Communication is about people. Cater to their needs, and good communication will follow.
